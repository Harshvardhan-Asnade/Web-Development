Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a superhero and one of the most iconic characters in the Marvel Universe. Created by writer Stan Lee, artist Don Heck, and editor Jack Kirby, Iron Man first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. The character was introduced as a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor who dons a high-tech suit of armor to save his life and ultimately become a protector of the world.
